# NestLauncher

NestLauncher is an Android Launcher that replicates the clean, minimalist design and functionality of Google's Nest Hub home screen on any Android device.

## About

NestLauncher brings the elegant and intuitive interface of the Nest Hub to your Android phone or tablet. It provides a streamlined home screen experience focused on essentials, with a card-based layout that displays useful information and quick access to your favorite apps.

## Features

Nest Hub-inspired Design: Clean, minimalist interface with a card-based layout
Quick Access: Easy-to-reach shortcuts for your most-used apps and functions
Information Cards: Display relevant information at a glance
Responsive Interface: Smooth and intuitive navigation
Customizable: Personalize your launcher to suit your preferences
